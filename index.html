Our Programs

Available Trades

All programs are affiliated with the Trade Testing Board Punjab (TTBP). Certificates are recognized nationally and internationally.

DAE · 36 Months

Electrical Technology

Power systems, motor control, industrial wiring, and electrical installations. Prepares graduates for WAPDA, IESCO, and overseas employment.

Qualification: Matric Jan & Aug Intake
DAE · 36 Months

Electronics Technology

Digital circuits, microcontrollers, communication systems, and consumer electronics. Highest enrollment program — 150 seats per batch.

Qualification: Matric Jan & Aug Intake
G-II · 24 Months

Electronics Application

Radio, TV, and consumer electronics repair. Practical troubleshooting and circuit diagnosis with hands-on workshop training.

Qualification: 8th Pass Jan & Aug Intake
G-II · 24 Months

Auto Mechanic

Engine overhaul, fuel injection, auto electrical, and transmission systems. High demand in local workshops and Gulf/Saudi markets.

Qualification: 8th Pass Jan & Aug Intake
G-II · 24 Months

Draftsman Civil (AutoCAD)

Construction drawings, structural layouts, and site plans using AutoCAD software. Demand in NHA, WAPDA, and construction firms.

Qualification: Matric Jan & Aug Intake
G-III · 12 Months

Welder

Arc, MIG/TIG welding, gas cutting, and pipe fabrication. One of Pakistan's most in-demand trades for Gulf and Middle East employment.

Qualification: 8th Pass Jan & Aug Intake
G-III · 12 Months

Electrician

Domestic and industrial wiring, panel boards, and electrical safety. Qualifies for WAPDA, construction sites, and self-employment.

Qualification: 8th Pass Jan & Aug Intake
Certificate · 6–12 Months

Beautician

Hair care, skin treatment, makeup artistry, and salon management. Ideal for women seeking self-employment or a professional beauty career.

Qualification: Open Entry Flexible Intake
About the Institute

Serving Mianwali for Decades

Govt. Technical Training Institute Mianwali is operated by TEVTA (Technical Education & Vocational Training Authority), Government of Punjab. Located on Talagang Road, near Govt. Degree College, Wandhi Arayanwali.

We serve students from Mianwali, Bhakkar, Kundian, Isakhel, and surrounding areas. All trade certificates are issued by the Trade Testing Board Punjab (TTBP) — recognized nationally and in Gulf countries.

  • TEVTA Punjab — Government of Punjab Certified
  • TTBP Certified Certificates for all trades
  • DAE (3yr), G-II (2yr), G-III (1yr) programs
  • Practical workshop training with industry tools
  • Principal: Engr. Farhan Ahmad
  • Contact: 0459-233829
How to Apply

Admission Process

Simple four-step process to enroll. Sessions open twice a year — January and August.

1

Choose Your Trade

Select from 8+ TEVTA trades based on your interest and qualification. Visit or call for guidance.

2

Submit Documents

B-Form / CNIC, school certificates, and passport photos at the admission office.

3

Pay Admission Fee

Affordable government fee structure. Concessions available for deserving students.

4

Begin Training

Start practical and theoretical classes in on-campus workshops with experienced instructors.

Contact

Get in Touch

For admissions, fee structure, trade availability, or any queries — contact the institute directly.
